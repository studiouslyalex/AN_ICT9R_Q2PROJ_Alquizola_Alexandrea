OUR PURPOSE


Known as the "Ate ng Bayan," creator and founder Alexandrea "Alex" Alquizola created Alex's Fantastic Ice Cream to showcase the beauty of Filipino-style ice-cream bounded by rich and delicate ingredients. Indeed, the classic flavorings will forever capture your fantastic feelings through this iconic and memorable ice cream project shop!


It is also known to be shown in several features for its authenticity, innocence, and purity throughout countless projects.

OUR OFFERINGS


  • A detailed description of products and ingredients embedded in notes
  • A diversified array of sweet delicacies from our belvoed fruits, spices, and plants
  • A minimalist web design for easy access to procedures to create our signature flavors
  • An orderly step-by-step list of recipe instructions crafted from the heart
  • An open space for discovering new techniques and produces to create new or expanded ice cream combinations
