Made of granulated sugar and gradulated salt, the Salted Caramel Supreme invites a traditional mismatch of sweet and salty flavors essential to the tongue to open new horizons for openness and purity among yummy ingredients.

Ingredients:
Quantity Ingredient
60ml Cooled salted caramel syrup
400ml Condensed milk
3pcs. Eggs
400ml Heavy cream
20ml Lemon juice
40g Granulated salt
100g Granulated sugar

Steps:
  1. In a medium saucepan set over medium-high heat, bring the milk, heavy cream, and sugar just to a boil while stirring occasionally.

  2. In a medium bowl, whisk the eggs until frothy.

  3. Slowly add half the hot milk/cream mixture to the eggs, whisking constantly; then pour this mixture back into the saucepan. Afterward, over medium-low heat, constantly stir the custard until it thickens enough to coat a spoon's back; it should never come to a boil. The custard should register 170Â°F on a digital thermometer when it is thick enough.

  4. Pour the custard through a fine-mesh strainer into a large bowl (a metal bowl is best). Set it aside.

  5. Warm the mixture over medium-low heat, stirring to help soften the caramel. Cook and stir until completely smooth.

  6. Stir the warm caramel into the warm custard completely, leaving no white streaks. The lighter and airy the cream, the silkier the ice cream.

  7. Transfer the mixture into a loaf tin and wrap it with plastic. Freeze the mixture for at least six hours or leave it overnight until it is firm.

  8. When the ice cream is firm, drizzle the mixture with the cooled salted caramel syrup.
