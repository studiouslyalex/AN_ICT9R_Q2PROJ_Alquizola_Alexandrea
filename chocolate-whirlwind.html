Made from the fresh shells of avocado and the sweet liquid of milk, the Avocado Amazement is a fruitilicious delicacy that encompasses calmness and peacefulness from its green emphasis.

Ingredients:
Quantity Ingredient
8pcs. Kitkat chocolate bars
400ml Condensed milk
400ml Heavy cream

Steps:
  1. Slice and crush the chocolate into smaller pieces. Afterward, transfer it into a clear bowl.

  2. Blend the chocolate with condensed milk in a food processor until the mixture is smooth. Afterward, transfer it into a large bowl.

  3. Beat the heavy cream in a large bowl using a hand mixer until stiff peaks form. Start beating the cream at low speed to build smaller bubbles for a stable foam. When the mixture begins to thicken, increase the speed to medium and continue to beat until you reach stiff peaks.

  4. Gently fold in the whipped cream to the chocolate mixture until there are no visible white streaks. The lighter and airy the whipped cream, the silkier the ice cream. Add the blended chocolate to the whipped cream in thirds and fold in gently with a flexible spatula to prevent the whipped cream from deflating.

  5. Transfer the mixture into a loaf tin and wrap it with plastic. Then, press it down onto its surface on a clean table to restrict it from creating ice crystals from within.

  6. Freeze the mixture for at least six hours or leave it overnight until it is firm.

  7. When the mixture is firm, decorate it with bits or slices of excess chocolate bars.
